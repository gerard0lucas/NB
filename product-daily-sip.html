What's Inside

1. Electrolytes

Sodium · Potassium · Chloride

These minerals carry an electric charge and are responsible for the body's fluid balance and electrical conductivity.

  • Rapid Fluid Absorption: Sodium and chloride transport water at the cellular level faster than water alone.
  • Muscle & Nerve Function: Potassium helps muscles contract and relax smoothly, lowering cramping risk.
  • Cellular Balance: Helps regulate blood volume and hemostatic levels, including cardiac rhythm.

2. Vitamins

Vitamin C · B3 · B6 · B12

Essential vitamins that support energy metabolism, cellular defence, and overall vitality.

  • Vitamin C: Powerful antioxidant supporting immune health and recovery from stress.
  • B-Complex (B3, B6, B12): Crucial cofactors that convert food into usable cellular energy and reduce fatigue.

How to Use

  1. 1

    Open one sachet

    Tear open a single Daily Sip sachet — one serving per day.

  2. 2

    Mix with water

    Pour into 250–300 ml of cold or room-temperature water. Stir or shake well until fully dissolved.

  3. 3

    Enjoy daily

    Best consumed in the morning or post-workout. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
