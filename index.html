FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our dietary supplement powders.

What is Daily Sip?

Daily Sip is Nutribuy's daily wellness supplement powder. It supports energy, calm, cognition, gut health, fibres, antioxidants, and daily nutritional requirements — in one palatable sachet per day.

What is Hydration Refreshica?

Hydration Refreshica is an electrolyte supplement powder with sodium, potassium, chloride, and essential vitamins. It's designed for rapid hydration during workouts, illness recovery, or long travel.

How do I take it?

Open one sachet, mix with 250–300 ml of water, stir or shake well, and drink. Take one serving per day unless advised otherwise by a healthcare professional.

Can I take both products?

Many customers use Daily Sip for morning wellness and Refreshica for hydration around activity. Consult your physician if you have medical conditions or take medication.

Does this replace drinking water?

No. These powders should not entirely replace your daily plain water intake unless recommended by a healthcare professional.

Is Nutribuy vegetarian?

Our supplement powders are formulated as vegetarian dietary supplements. Check individual product labels for full ingredient details.

Disclaimer: These products are dietary supplements and are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Consult your physician before use if pregnant, nursing, or on medication.
